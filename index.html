Macro environment and digital landscape

Australia remains one of the leading global adopters of the smartphone and 88 percent of Australians now own one, with market growth being driven by older generations. Statistic shows people check their phones 35 times a day on average. 35% of them check their phones within 5 minutes of waking up in the morning, with 70% using phones during mealtimes with family and friends.

Less users are now willing to download apps

Mobile device real estate is becoming increasingly scarce, as users are slimming down on mobile apps. The trend shows users are less interested in downloading apps. On the opposite side, mobile wallet creates an instant and lasting communication channel outside of the app for brands, with unparalleled install and retention rates. More than 85% of the digital passes are never removed.

More and more in-store transactions are finalised via mobile wallet

Mobile wallet usage is increasing at a fast rate (12% month to month, from Nov 2017 to Mar 2019). With banking industry embraces the technology, more and more customers have cards in their mobile wallet. Assume adoption rate maintain at this speed, June 2020 will show 43% transaction done via mobile wallet.
